<h1 align="center">Lime vanilla pack</h1>

a **HUMONGOUS** QoL modpack (+150 mods) for lethal company while keeping it vanilla, Basic utils do not have weight or take item slots, Basic cosmetics, XP system & missions, Enhanced enemy AI, Lag fix, custom boombox/TV/soundboards and much more!

## Download with custom Config

Latest Thunderstore profile : https://github.com/TheLime1/Lime_vanilla_pack/releases/latest

<details>
  <summary>What i changed in the config</summary>
  
  - kept the vanilla spawn rules for the Masked, unless you get a speicial mission to kill 4 of them.
    - (if you die while the mask was in your inventory, there is a chance to spawn a masked enemy)
  - Nerfed the ground worm.
  - synced voice mimic for several enemies.
    - (only factory enemies can mimic the player voice)
    - while you are alive you cant hear your own voice mimic, unless you are dead. (better emersion)
  - nerfed glow steps duration.
  - removed all plushies, kept only vanilla like scrap.
  - nerfed herobrine spawn rate.
  - nerfed the fps of the helmet cameras. (for better performance)
  - buffed the walkie-talkie battery. 

  
</details>
